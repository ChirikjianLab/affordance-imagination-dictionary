Requested Affordance: Providing compartmentalized storage spaces for organizing or holding items

If it is a cubby, it should afford containment and separation of multiple small objects within a set of open-faced compartments or cavities, typically arranged in a grid or array. Then we can use a placement test by inserting individual objects of appropriate size into each compartment and observing whether they remain stably held without rolling or falling out under static conditions.
