Class of object: Ladle

Requested Affordance: Scooping and holding liquid or semi-liquid materials for transfer

If it is a ladle, it should afford containment of a small amount of fluid in a concave bowl component at one end while being supported by a long handle for reaching into deeper containers. Then we can use a scooping-and-hold test by submerging the concave end in liquid, lifting it, and observing whether the liquid remains temporarily held in the bowl under static upright conditions. If the liquid stays contained without significant spillage while stationary, then it has the affordance of the requested class.
